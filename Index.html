My WhatsApp Strategy

One of the most underrated tools in today’s digital world is WhatsApp marketing—especially status views. While many ignore it, I’ve mastered how to use it to create real visibility and conversion.

Let’s break it down:
3 influencers × 5k–7k views = 15k–21k views/day
3× a week = 45k–63k views/week
In a month: 180k–252k views

Even if just 2% take action, that's 3,600–5,040 potential clicks or sales. No bots. Just real humans.
